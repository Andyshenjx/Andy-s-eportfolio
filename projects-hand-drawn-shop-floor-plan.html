Project

Hand Drawn Shop Floor Plan

Scaled shop layout, equipment labels, and measurement-based planning.

Goal

Goal

  • Learn how to draw the shop layout to scale.
  • Identify and label the equipment and safety features in the shop.
  • Practice measuring a large room and placing objects accurately on a floor plan.

Tools Used

Tools Used

  • Graph paper
  • Ruler
  • Tape measure
  • Pencil

What I Did

What I Did

  • Measured the perimeter dimensions of the shop.
  • Used graph paper to create a scaled floor plan.
  • Drew the approximate shape and location of shop equipment.
  • Labeled the equipment and safety features.
  • Worked out a scale so each square represented a certain number of feet.

Skills & Reflection

Skills & Reflection

  • I learned how to use measurements to create a scaled drawing.
  • I improved my ability to estimate locations and draw objects in the correct position.
  • This project helped me understand shop layout, equipment location, and how measurement is used in real workplace planning.
