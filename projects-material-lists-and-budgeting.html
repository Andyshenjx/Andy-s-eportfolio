Project

Material Lists and Budgeting

Material planning, price research, and cost estimation for a manufactured product.

Goal

Goal

  • Learn how to create a material list and estimate project costs.
  • Identify the materials, hardware, and supplies needed for a manufactured product.
  • Develop budgeting and planning skills used in manufacturing projects.

Tools Used

Tools Used

  • Tape Measure
  • Calculator
  • Online Supplier Websites

What I Did

What I Did

  • Reverse engineered a park bench by identifying its components and dimensions.
  • Created a material list for the steel frame, lumber, hardware, and paint.
  • Researched material prices from online suppliers.
  • Calculated the total project cost using a spreadsheet.

Skills & Reflection

Skills & Reflection

  • I learned how to create a bill of materials and estimate manufacturing costs.
  • I improved my measurement and planning skills.
  • This project showed me how budgeting is an important part of manufacturing and project planning.
