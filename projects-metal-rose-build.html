Project

Metal Rose Build

Plasma cutting, shaping petals, welding, and assembling a decorative metal rose.

Goal

Goal

  • Learn how to fabricate and assemble a decorative metal rose.
  • Practice welding techniques.
  • Create a finished project from individual metal components.

Tools Used

Tools Used

  • CNC Plasma Cutter
  • Welding Equipment
  • Large Pliers
  • Wrenches
  • Safety Equipment

What I Did

What I Did

  • Cut the metal petals and leaves using a CNC plasma cutter.
  • Bent five petals into shape using large pliers.
  • Formed the leaves and stem components.
  • Attached the petals to the stem using welding.
  • Adjusted the shape of the flower to create a realistic appearance.

Safety

Safety

  • Used welding equipment safely and followed shop procedures.
  • Kept hands clear of hot metal after cutting and welding.
  • Handled sharp metal edges carefully during assembly.

Skills & Reflection

Skills & Reflection

  • I learned how to shape metal by bending and forming it into a final design.
  • I gained experience using a CNC plasma cutter and welding equipment.
  • I practiced assembling multiple parts into a finished project.
