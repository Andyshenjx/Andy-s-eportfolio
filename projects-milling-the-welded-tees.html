Project

Milling the Welded Tees

Vertical milling machine setup, controlled cutting, and surface preparation.

Goal

Goal

  • Learn how to operate a vertical milling machine safely and accurately.
  • Remove old weld material from a welded tee and prepare the surface for future work.

Tools Used

Tools Used

  • Vertical Milling Machine
  • Milling Cutter
  • Machine Vise
  • Safety Glasses

What I Did

What I Did

  • Secured the welded tee in the machine vise.
  • Positioned the workpiece before machining.
  • Adjusted the spindle speed and checked the spindle rotation direction.
  • Removed old weld material using multiple milling passes.
  • Checked the surface finish and accuracy throughout the process.

Safety

Safety

  • Wore safety glasses while operating the milling machine.
  • Ensured the workpiece was securely clamped before cutting.
  • Kept hands away from moving machine components.
  • Used the spindle brake correctly when stopping the machine.

Skills & Reflection

Skills & Reflection

  • I learned how to operate a vertical milling machine and make controlled cuts.
  • I practiced controlling the X and Y axis movements to achieve accurate results.
  • I gained experience setting spindle speeds and securing workpieces properly.
  • This project showed me the importance of precision, patience, and safety when machining metal.
